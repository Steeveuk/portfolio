Frequently Asked Questions

What is a QR Code?

A QR Code (short for Quick Response Code) is a type of matrix barcode that contains information such as text, URLs, or other data. QR Codes are often used to quickly and easily share information between devices.

How do I use a QR Code?

To use a QR Code, you will need a QR Code reader or scanner app on your device. You can then hold the camera up to the QR Code and the app will detect and interpret the code, allowing you to access the information it contains.

How do I create a QR Code?

You can create a QR Code using our QR Code generator tool. This tool allows you to enter a URL to generate a QR Code. You can then save the QR Code as an image and share it with others.

What can I use a QR Code for?

QR Codes can be used for a wide range of purposes, including sharing information, tracking inventory, and providing access to digital content. They are also commonly used in marketing and advertising campaigns to engage with customers and provide them with additional information about a product or service.
