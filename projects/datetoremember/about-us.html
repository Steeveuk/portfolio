Date to Remember host exciting singles events that bring people together in fun and relaxed settings. Whether it's a thrilling night of games, a friendly bowling competition, a captivating museum tour, a scenic hiking adventure, or a night filled with live music, we offer a variety of activities designed to help you connect, laugh, and make lasting memories.
People are tired of swiping through endless profiles, hoping to find someone real. It's so easy to get stuck in the cycle of judging people based on a photo or a few lines of text. We all know deep down, there is a better way to connect.

There is hope and through in-person events where you're doing things you love, forming genuine connections. You get to have real conversations, see how someone truly interacts and feel the spark in the moment. No waiting around for replies or worrying about authenticity, just real, face-to-face connections that could lead to something meaningful. Wouldn't that be a better way to meet someone special?

The Who and Why behind Date to Remember

Our team is made up of three Leeds based people who have all seen how dating apps don't work for most and recognise the need for another way for single people to mingle. We are creating safe spaces so you feel comfortable at our events and can meet new faces with the support of hosts. All events are ticketed and attendees are checked in. Our hosts are there to look after your needs, make sure you are at ease, chat about all things dating and be there as a friendly face!

So come join us for an unforgettable experience, you never know who you might meet!
